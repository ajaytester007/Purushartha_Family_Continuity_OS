# Purushartha Family Continuity OS

A consent-first relationship, marriage, family, parenting, continuity, resilience, wealth, and legacy intelligence framework organized around Dharma, Artha, Kama, and Moksha.

## Mission

Help individuals and families make better decisions across the full life journey: self-readiness, courtship, commitment, marriage, household operations, intimacy, parenting, giftedness and special support needs, wealth, crisis, breakup, divorce, bereavement, new-partner entry, midlife, aging, caregiving, and intergenerational legacy.

The system distinguishes:
- a hard life that is flourishing from an easy life that is quietly failing;
- correctable immaturity from patterned harm;
- incompatibility from wrongdoing;
- temporary sacrifice from chronic exploitation;
- continuity of a life vision from replacement of a human being.

## Non-Negotiable Principles

1. No secret surveillance.
2. No diagnosis from a single artifact.
3. No permanent moral score for a human being.
4. Safety outranks continuity.
5. Children are not assets, rescuers, or return-on-investment vehicles.
6. A new partner is a co-author, not a substitute employee.
7. Good deeds do not purchase permission for abuse.
8. Sensitive evidence is minimized, access-controlled, and purpose-limited.
9. Automated outputs are hypotheses requiring human review.
10. The primary goal is wiser action, not winning a case.

## System Flow

Ingest with consent → Normalize → Build timeline → Detect patterns → Separate fact from inference → Assess impact and repair → Update bounded state → Visualize → Recommend next action → Measure outcome → Review at tollgate.

## Quick Start

1. Complete `09_Templates/Purushartha_Life_Audit_Template.md`.
2. Complete `09_Templates/Partner_Maturity_Assessment_Template.md` separately for each partner.
3. Log one event using `Relationship_Event_Log_Template.md`.
4. Classify it using the incident library.
5. Apply the scoring rules without treating the result as a verdict.
6. Run the relevant tollgate.
7. Choose one playbook and define a retest date.
8. Record the outcome, not merely the intention.

## Disclaimer

This framework is educational decision support, not medical, legal, financial, mental-health, child-development, or emergency advice. In danger, coercive control, threats, violence, stalking, or child-safety concerns, prioritize qualified local support and immediate safety over relationship optimization.
