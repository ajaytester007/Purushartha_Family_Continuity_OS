# Case 01 — First Failure and Repair

Synthetic case.

A partner fails to help during a stressful household task. The expectation was partly implicit. After feedback, the partner initially defends the behavior, later acknowledges the impact, asks for a clearer shared-help rule, and shows initiative for 30 days.

Classification: Level 2 maturity failure.
Responsibility: primarily the non-helping partner, moderated by implicit expectations.
Action: Correct.
Retest: 30 days.
Success: spontaneous noticing and reliable follow-through.
