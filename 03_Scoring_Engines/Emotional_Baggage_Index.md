# Emotional Baggage Index

Tracks unresolved load, not personal worth.

Domains:
- past relationship residue;
- family-of-origin conflict;
- grief;
- shame;
- resentment;
- fear and avoidance;
- unresolved current injuries.

Rate load 0–5, activation frequency 0–5, spillover 0–5, and active repair 0–5.

Priority = load × activation × spillover ÷ (1 + repair).

Never infer trauma or diagnosis from messages alone.
