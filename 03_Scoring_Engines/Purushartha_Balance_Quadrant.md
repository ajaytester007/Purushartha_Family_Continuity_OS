# Purushartha Balance Quadrant

Score Dharma, Artha, Kama, Moksha 0–100 with evidence.

## Magic Quadrant
High Vision / High Execution: Flourishing
High Vision / Low Execution: Dream-rich, system-poor
Low Vision / High Execution: Efficient, directionless
Low Vision / Low Execution: Fragile/drifting

Overlay the four Purusharthas and identify domination risk.
