# Family Complexity-Adjusted Quality of Life

Report both raw and adjusted views.

## Raw QoL
Health, rest, connection, financial comfort, environment, autonomy, joy.

## Complexity Load
Care needs, gifted opportunities, illness, disability, elder care, multiple children, crisis, relocation, work intensity.

## Adjustment Principle
Do not hide suffering. Explain whether lower comfort reflects chosen productive sacrifice, unavoidable burden, or failing systems.

Output:
- Raw QoL
- Complexity Load
- Adjusted Interpretation
- Protected Floors
- Burnout Risk
- Sibling/Partner Fairness
- Sustainability Horizon
