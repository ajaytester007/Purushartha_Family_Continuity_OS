# Family Wealth Management

## Six Buckets
1. Survival
2. Stability
3. Growth
4. Joy
5. Luxury
6. Legacy

## Household Capital
Track:
- financial;
- health;
- intellectual;
- relational;
- reputation;
- cultural;
- spiritual;
- infrastructure;
- professional;
- community capital.

## Financial Torts
Secret debt, concealed spending, coercive restriction, unilateral high-risk bets, chronic failure to plan, and using money to control access or movement.

## Resilience Ladder
Cash flow → emergency reserve → insurance → debt discipline → diversified growth → succession and legacy.

Any implementation involving investments, taxes, insurance, prenups, or estates should be reviewed by qualified professionals.
