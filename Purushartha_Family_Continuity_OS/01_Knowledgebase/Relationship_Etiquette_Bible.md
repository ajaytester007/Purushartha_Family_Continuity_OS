# Relationship Etiquette Bible

## Core Doctrine
Judge patterns by conduct, context, impact, repair, recurrence, and learning.

## Etiquette Ladder
1. Notice.
2. Ask.
3. Clarify.
4. Consider impact.
5. Act proportionately.
6. Repair quickly.
7. Learn visibly.

## Everyday Rules
- Do not humiliate in public.
- Do not use confidences as weapons.
- Do not force immediate resolution during dysregulation.
- Do not make major joint decisions unilaterally.
- Do not recruit audiences to win ordinary couple conflicts.
- Keep promises or renegotiate before failure.
- Ask before sharing private relationship details.
- Express appreciation for ordinary labor.
- Make requests specific and answerable.
- Distinguish “I dislike this” from “you are bad.”

## Conflict Rule
A conflict is not complete when talking stops. It is complete when the issue is understood, practical repair is agreed, and follow-through is reviewed.

## Elimination Rule
Never eliminate a potentially good partner for one correctable first failure. Never retain a repeatedly harmful partner merely because of history, chemistry, investment, or social pressure.
