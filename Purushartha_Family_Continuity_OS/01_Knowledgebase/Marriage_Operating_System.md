# Marriage Operating System

## Weekly
- 20-minute logistics review.
- One appreciation each.
- One unresolved issue maximum for structured discussion.
- Protect one shared restorative activity.

## Monthly
Review money, household burden, intimacy/affection, health, family obligations, and upcoming stress.

## Quarterly
Run:
- Purushartha audit;
- burden skew review;
- financial resilience review;
- shared joy portfolio;
- drift detector.

## Annual
Revisit:
- beneficiaries and insurance;
- emergency plan;
- wills/estate documents with professionals;
- career geography;
- child/pet/elder obligations;
- major dreams;
- relationship renewal plan.

## Decision Rights
Classify decisions:
- individual;
- inform;
- consult;
- joint consent;
- emergency authority.

Ambiguity about decision rights is a recurring source of avoidable conflict.
