# Quick Escape from Anxiety

## Steps
1. Name the decision that actually needs to be made.
2. Separate today’s facts from feared futures.
3. Delay non-urgent irreversible action for one sleep cycle.
4. Choose one stabilizing action: food, sleep, walk, support call, logistics.
5. Set a specific review time so rumination does not become the plan.

## Outcome
Record what changed, what did not, and the next review date.
