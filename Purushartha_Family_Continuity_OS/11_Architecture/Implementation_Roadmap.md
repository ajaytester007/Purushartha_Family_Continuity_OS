# Implementation Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Constitution
Finalize rights, consent, evidence standards, safety overrides.

## Phase 1 — Knowledge and Manual MVP
Books, templates, event logs, scoring sheets, Mermaid maps.

## Phase 2 — Structured Data
JSON schemas, SCD2 state, synthetic cases, validation tests.

## Phase 3 — Consent-Safe Ingestion
User-submitted correspondence and narratives; provenance and permissions.

## Phase 4 — Explainable Intelligence
Pattern detection, contradiction handling, confidence, repair tracking.

## Phase 5 — Experience Layer
Dashboards, mind maps, quick scripts, tollgate reviews.

## Phase 6 — Family Continuity
Crisis modes, interregnum, new-partner entry, blended/joint family.

## Phase 7 — Wealth and Legacy
Resilience, succession, Purushartha balance.

## Phase 8 — Validation
Independent review by relationship, privacy, safety, child-development, legal, financial, and cultural experts.

## MVP Acceptance Criteria
- no hidden raw evidence;
- every score explainable;
- every decision reversible where possible;
- safety override tested;
- synthetic bias tests;
- deletion and consent tests;
- no partner replacement logic that erases personhood.
