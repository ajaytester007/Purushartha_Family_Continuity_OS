# Individual Partnership Maturity Score (0–100)

Self-awareness 10; Empathy 10; Honesty 10; Accountability 15; Repair 15; Boundaries 10; Reliability 10; Regulation 10; Adaptability 10.

## Evidence Scale
0 absent/harmful; 1 rare; 2 inconsistent; 3 adequate; 4 strong; 5 exemplary.

Convert weighted dimensions to 100.

## Rules
- Use a rolling window plus lifetime pattern notes.
- Confidence must be shown: Low/Medium/High.
- No score from fewer than three meaningful contexts.
- Safety violations are separate overrides.
- Improvement slope matters more than tiny score differences.

## Output
Score, confidence, strongest evidence, contradiction, trend, next behavior to test.
