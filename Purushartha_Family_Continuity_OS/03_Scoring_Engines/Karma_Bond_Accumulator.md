# Karma Bond Accumulator

Karma here means accumulated relational consequences, not supernatural punishment.

## Ledgers
**Goodwill Capital:** reliability, care, sacrifice freely given, celebration, protection, repair.
**Emotional Debt:** unresolved betrayal, dismissal, contempt, broken promises, abandonment, boundary violations.
**Repair Credits:** tangible restitution plus sustained behavior change.
**Uncertainty Reserve:** disputed or weakly evidenced events.

## Event Update
Severity × foreseeability × responsibility × recurrence multiplier.
Then subtract validated repair, capped so repair cannot erase history or safety concerns.

## Decay
Minor repaired incidents fade in decision weight over time.
Severe harms do not disappear merely because time passes.

## Anti-Gaming
- good deeds cannot offset abuse;
- gifts do not equal repair;
- apologies without change earn minimal credit;
- score manipulation is itself a governance concern.
