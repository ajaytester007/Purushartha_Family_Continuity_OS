# Relationship Health Score

Dimensions: Safety, Trust, Reciprocity, Affection, Respect, Teamwork, Repair, Growth.

Score each 0–100. Use the geometric mean so one collapsed dimension cannot be hidden by excellence elsewhere.

## Interpretation
85–100 flourishing
70–84 healthy with work
55–69 strained
40–54 high-risk deterioration
below 40 critical review

These bands are decision aids, not diagnoses. Safety overrides the composite.
