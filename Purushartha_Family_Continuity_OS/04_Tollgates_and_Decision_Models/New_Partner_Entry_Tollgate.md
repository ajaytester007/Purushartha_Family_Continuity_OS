# New Partner Entry Tollgate

## Purpose
Structured decision support for new partner entry.

## Questions
- [ ] Prior relationship ethically closed?
- [ ] Grief/recovery adequate?
- [ ] Continuity brief consent-safe?
- [ ] New partner has renegotiation rights?
- [ ] Children/dependents paced appropriately?
- [ ] Comparison to predecessor contained?

## Decision Rule
Record evidence for and against each answer. Identify uncertainty. Choose the least irreversible action that adequately protects safety and dignity.

## Outputs
Decision; rationale; dissenting view; safeguards; next review date; measurable outcome.

## Override
Safety concerns bypass ordinary optimization and move to protection planning.
