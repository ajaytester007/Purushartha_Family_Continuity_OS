# Divorce or Recovery Tollgate

## Purpose
Structured decision support for recovery versus separation.

## Questions
- [ ] Safety?
- [ ] Pattern severity?
- [ ] Repair history?
- [ ] Children/dependents impact?
- [ ] Financial/legal facts reviewed?
- [ ] Is continued exposure causing cumulative harm?

## Decision Rule
Record evidence for and against each answer. Identify uncertainty. Choose the least irreversible action that adequately protects safety and dignity.

## Outputs
Decision; rationale; dissenting view; safeguards; next review date; measurable outcome.

## Override
Safety concerns bypass ordinary optimization and move to protection planning.
