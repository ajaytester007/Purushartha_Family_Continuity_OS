# Case 02 — Gifted Child and Family Capture

Synthetic case.

One child's competitive pathway consumes weekends, travel budget, and one parent's attention. Another child becomes quiet and highly independent. Couple time collapses.

Raw QoL falls. Opportunity outcomes rise.

Diagnosis: productive sacrifice drifting toward family capture.
Action: protect sibling time, rotate travel, seek funding, restore couple floor, set a six-month review.
