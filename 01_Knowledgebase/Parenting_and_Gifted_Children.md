# Parenting and Gifted Children

## Parallel Child Journeys
Each child has an independent stream:
conception → pregnancy → infant → toddler → elementary → middle → high school → college/launch → adult child.

## Exceptional Progeny Complexity
Track:
- talent domain;
- developmental asynchrony;
- coaching/education needs;
- time demand;
- travel;
- financial load;
- sibling effects;
- parental identity loss;
- opportunity windows;
- burnout.

## Four States
- Temporary Compression
- Productive Sacrifice
- Chronic Overextension
- Family Capture

## Tollgate Question
Are we nurturing exceptional potential, or has the opportunity begun consuming the family that exists to nurture it?

## Required Floors
Couple connection, sibling fairness, solvency, health, and child autonomy.
