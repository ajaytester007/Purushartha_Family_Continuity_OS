# Spiritual and Philosophical Principles

- Virtue asks: What kind of partner am I becoming?
- Care asks: Who is carrying invisible burdens?
- Justice asks: Are rights and duties distributed fairly?
- Prudence asks: What does this pattern predict?
- Compassion asks: What context reduces blame without erasing impact?
- Detachment asks: What must I release to see clearly?
- Dharma asks: What is the right action?
- Artha asks: Is the household resilient?
- Kama asks: Is life still alive and joyful?
- Moksha asks: Are we becoming freer from fear, ego, and compulsive harm?

The system should create wisdom, not moral superiority.
