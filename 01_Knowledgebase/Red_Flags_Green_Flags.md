# Red Flags, Green Flags and Pattern Flags

## Green
Curiosity, accountability, reliability, respect for boundaries, proportional conflict, visible learning.

## Yellow
Defensiveness, avoidance, poor initiative, inconsistent follow-through, family enmeshment—when acknowledged and improving.

## Orange
Repeated deception, contempt, retaliation for feedback, chronic boundary erosion, financial secrecy, isolation attempts.

## Red
Violence, threats, coercive control, stalking, severe intimidation, sexual coercion, child endangerment.

## Pattern Rule
One artifact rarely establishes a pattern. Repetition, cross-context consistency, escalation, and failed repair matter.
