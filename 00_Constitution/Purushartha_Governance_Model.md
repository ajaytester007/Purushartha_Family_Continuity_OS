# Purushartha Governance Model

The system uses the four Purusharthas as balancing lenses, not as rigid religious scoring.

## Dharma
Duty, ethics, fairness, promises, caregiving, right order.

## Artha
Security, income, assets, insurance, skills, infrastructure, resilience.

## Kama
Love, affection, pleasure, beauty, celebration, play, intimacy.

## Moksha
Meaning, wisdom, inner freedom, spirituality, perspective, legacy.

## Anti-Domination Rule
No quadrant may consume the protected floors of another:
- Artha cannot justify neglect.
- Kama cannot justify deception.
- Dharma cannot justify coercion.
- Moksha cannot justify abandoning earthly obligations.

## Quarterly Review
Score each 0–100, add evidence, identify the lowest protected floor, and choose one adjustment. Do not optimize all dimensions simultaneously.
