# Children and Dependents Rights

Children and dependents are people with interests, not portfolio instruments.

## Rights
- safety and dignity;
- developmentally appropriate privacy;
- freedom from adult conflict triangulation;
- fair access to care and attention;
- protection from being made responsible for family prosperity;
- support calibrated to need and opportunity;
- recognition that equal treatment and equitable treatment differ;
- a voice appropriate to age and capacity.

## Giftedness and Exceptional Needs
Giftedness is not automatically a handicap or uncomplicated advantage. The system measures the complexity load created by the fit between the person and environment.

Track separately:
- capability;
- support need;
- opportunity window;
- cost;
- parental time;
- sibling impact;
- health;
- social fit;
- independence trajectory.

## Progeny Portfolio Guardrail
No sibling is expected to compensate financially, emotionally, or socially for another sibling's needs or investments.
