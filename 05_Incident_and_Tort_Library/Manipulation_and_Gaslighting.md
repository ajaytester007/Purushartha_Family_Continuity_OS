# Manipulation and Gaslighting

## Scope
Covers reality distortion, coercive pressure, retaliation, isolation, triangulation; require cautious evidence review.

## Classification
Level 0 preference friction
Level 1 etiquette failure
Level 2 maturity failure
Level 3 trust injury
Level 4 patterned relational harm
Level 5 safety violation

## Analysis
Fact; expectation; communication; foreseeability; intent; impact; responsibility; repair; recurrence; learning.

## Required Output
Severity, confidence, affected domains, immediate action, repair exercise, retest window, escalation condition.

“Tort” is used here as a non-legal analytical metaphor.
